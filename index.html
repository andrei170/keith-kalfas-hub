The Three Bottlenecks

What is leaking, ranked by revenue impact.

Three gaps inside the current funnel. Each one is a separate install. They compound once all three are live.

Highest Revenue Lever
01

The cohort layer between the Alliance and the 1:1 coaching call is missing.

The Alliance does the community job at $97/mo. The courses do the low-ticket job at $97-$347. The 1:1 coaching call does the high-touch job at the top. There is no $2K-$5K structured group product in between. The operator who has paid for the bundle, listened to the podcast in the truck for a year, and is stuck at $80K-$200K revenue has two options: $97/mo for the Alliance (community, not structured implementation) or the 1:1 call (capped by Keith's hours). Neither is the right shape for an operator who wants a specific install timeline with a real deliverable at each stage.

A "Landscaping Operator Cohort" - 8 weeks, weekly group call with Keith, scheduled office hours, 20-25 seat cap - built around four installs the operator can sign his name to: pricing fixed (week 1-2), first crew lead hired (week 3-4), route economics locked (week 5-6), owner-out-of-the-truck plan (week 7-8). Not lessons. Decisions made and paperwork signed during the 8 weeks. The Alliance stays as the recurring layer; the cohort is the implementation upgrade, not the replacement.

Cost of Waiting

4-6 cohorts per year x 20-25 seats x mid-tier mentorship pricing = $160K-$750K Year-1 direct on the cohort line alone, plus Alliance retention lift from cohort grads who continue as Alliance members at the higher tier, plus 1:1 spillover from grads who want ongoing. Each quarter the cohort layer does not exist is one cycle missed.

6 months from now~$80K-$375K in cohort revenue not collected, plus the 36K IG audience and the podcast catalogue keep routing warm listeners to the $97/mo Alliance (under-sells the high-intent operator) or the 1:1 (capped by calendar). The middle buyer exits the funnel without spending.

Fix · Landscaping Operator Cohort + application gate. Year-1 floor $160K-$750K direct. Install time: 3-7 days from kickoff.
Fast Install
02

36K IG plus the podcast catalogue and no scorecard capturing email at the bio.

The lead magnets are PDFs: "7 Steps to Marketing Your Business," the Free Mini Marketing Course, the Brand Advantage Workshop. PDFs capture an email and deliver one piece of content. They do not diagnose. A year-one solo landscaper bidding $50/cut and a year-five operator running 3 trucks at $1M revenue both download the same PDF. The list cannot tell them apart. The next email goes to both. The product recommendation goes to both. Conversion math on every downstream offer gets compressed to the lowest common denominator.

A "Landscaping Business Trap Score" - 12 questions, returns a 0-100 score plus a 3-line bottleneck readout (which trap is costing the operator the most right now: pricing, hiring, route economics, or owner-as-bottleneck) - segments the inbound subscriber on capture and routes them to the right next product. Alliance for the community-stage operator. Bundle for the content-stage operator. Cohort for the implementation-stage operator. 1:1 call for the top-stage operator.

Cost of Waiting

5-8% IG-to-email is realistic on a high-intent trades audience with the right diagnostic = 2K-3K incremental segmented subscribers in 90 days from the existing IG base alone, 5K-8K in 6 months once the podcast end-card and YouTube end-card route to the scorecard. Year-1 lift: $60K-$160K incremental from scorecard-segmented sales across the existing product ladder. Compounds for life of the list.

6 months from now~$30K-$80K never captured, plus 6 months of segmentation data not collected. When the cohort opens, there is no warm pre-segmented list to launch to. The launch is harder and the cost-per-seat for Cohort 1 is higher.

Fix · Landscaping Business Trap Score on ScoreApp or Typeform. 2K-3K incremental graded subscribers in 90 days. Install time: 1-2 days.
Catalogue Compound
03

The Untrapped Podcast catalogue has no productised cohort waitlist or end-card upsell.

The podcast is on every major platform and has hundreds of episodes. Each episode is 30-60 minutes of listener time with Keith already in the truck cab. That is the warmest possible audience for the cohort - they are not casual scrollers, they are operators who listened to the whole episode. Right now the end-card on each episode is either no CTA, a generic "go to keithkalfas.com," or an affiliate plug. The cohort that does not yet exist is the natural product for that listener. The waitlist page is the asset that captures them between cohort cycles.

A "Cohort Waitlist" page - one-pager with the cohort overview, application form, cohort cycle dates, what gets installed by week 4 vs week 8 - sits at one short URL. The podcast end-card, the YouTube end-card, the IG bio router, and the podcast show notes all route here. Application is open year-round. Cohort cycles run quarterly. Alliance members get priority application. The waitlist is the implementation upgrade for listeners who want to do real work.

Cost of Waiting

Each month the waitlist does not exist, the podcast catalogue continues routing warm listeners to the Alliance or the 1:1. Year-1 floor from podcast and YouTube attributable cohort applications: $80K-$200K incremental. Compounds with every new episode shipped. The YouTube long-form catalogue has 10+ years of evergreen "how to start a landscaping business" content - each of those videos is a top-of-funnel asset that currently routes to nothing above the $347 bundle.

6 months from nowTwo cohort cycles missed on the podcast lane. Every future episode ships without the waitlist end-card = a leaked conversion from a listener who already invested 30-60 minutes.

Fix · Cohort Waitlist + podcast and YouTube end-card refresh on next 10 episodes. $80K-$200K Year-1 attributable. Install time: built in parallel with the cohort.
