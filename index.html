Highest Revenue Lever
01

The Cohort Layer Between The Alliance And The 1:1

The Alliance is community-stage at $97/mo. The 1:1 coaching call is the top of the stack, bottlenecked by your hours. Nothing sits in between. A "Landscaping Operator Cohort" - 8 weeks, weekly group call, 20-25 seat cap - built around four installs the operator can sign his name to: pricing fixed (week 1-2), first crew lead hired (week 3-4), route economics locked (week 5-6), owner-out-of-the-truck plan written (week 7-8). 4-6 cohorts per year x 20-25 seats x mid-tier mentorship pricing = $160K-$750K direct, plus Alliance retention lift and 1:1 spillover. Time cost on you is weekly group call (60-75 min) + 2x monthly office hours - not weekly content production.

Cost of Waiting

~$40-125K per cohort cycle not collected. Each quarter the mid-tier does not exist is 1-2 cohort cycles missed. The audience past the $347 bundle has nowhere to land except the Alliance (under-sells the mid-tier operator) or the 1:1 (caps you by the calendar).

6 months from now ·~$80-375K in cohort revenue never collected, plus the operator who would have paid $2K-$5K this quarter has either bought the bundle (low-ticket exit) or paid for a 1:1 (your-hour exit).

Fix · Landscaping Operator Cohort. Standard tier and Premium tier. Year-1 floor $160K-$600K direct + Alliance retention lift + 1:1 spillover.
Fast Install
02

36K IG + Podcast Catalogue, No Scorecard Capturing Email

Lead magnets are PDFs. PDFs deliver one piece of content and capture an email but they do not diagnose. A year-1 solo bidding $50/cut and a year-5 operator at 3 trucks both download the same PDF. The list cannot tell them apart. A "Landscaping Business Trap Score" - 12 questions, returns 0-100 score plus the trap costing the most right now (pricing, hiring, route, or owner-as-bottleneck) - captures email, segments on capture, and routes the subscriber to the right product. 5-8% IG-to-email is realistic on a high-intent trades audience = 2K-3K incremental subscribers in 90 days, 5K-8K in 6 months once podcast and YouTube end-cards route in.

Cost of Waiting

~$5-13K/mo in scorecard-segmented sales not happening across the existing product ladder. The cohort waitlist gets pitched to subscribers who should be on the Alliance, and the Alliance gets pitched to subscribers who are past it and should be in the cohort. Conversion math gets compressed at every tier.

6 months from now ·~$30-80K never captured + the email list is 6 months smaller and 6 months less segmented when the cohort opens.

Fix · Landscaping Business Trap Score on ScoreApp / Typeform. 2K-3K incremental subscribers in 90 days. $60K-$160K Year-1 lift. Compounds for life of the list.
Compound Pickup
03

The Untrapped Podcast Catalogue Routes To Nothing Productised

The podcast is on every major platform with hundreds of episodes. Each end-card is either no CTA, a generic "go to keithkalfas.com," or an affiliate plug. The catalogue is the warmest top-of-funnel asset in the niche - operators put it on in the truck and listen for 30-60 minutes. A "Cohort Waitlist" one-pager + podcast end-card refresh on next 10 episodes + YouTube end-card refresh on top 20 evergreens routes the warm listener to the mid-tier cohort. The compounding lift on a single end-card swap is real and currently unmeasured.

Cost of Waiting

~$7-17K/mo in podcast and YouTube-attributable cohort applications not happening. Each new episode shipped without a waitlist link in the end-card is a leaked conversion - the listener has invested 30-60 minutes, then has nowhere structured to land above the $347 bundle.

6 months from now ·Another 6 months of warm catalogue routing to nothing productised, and another 6 months of attribution data missed.

Fix · Cohort Waitlist one-page hub + podcast end-card refresh + YouTube end-card refresh on top 20. $80K-$200K Year-1 attributable. Compounds with every new episode.
