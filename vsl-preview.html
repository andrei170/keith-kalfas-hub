Phase 1

VSL Preview Script - Keith Kalfas

Phase 1 deliverable · Short-form preview script for the Landscaping Operator Cohort hero VSL. Page layout, production notes, distribution.

Short preview format. The full cohort sales VSL gets scoped in Phase 2 once cohort positioning is locked from the first 10 applicant calls.

Format

5-7 minute preview VSL hosted on Wistia, embedded in the landing-page hero. Trades-register, plain, decision-led. Single take where possible, supplemented by B-roll of a truck loading at sunrise, a real bid sheet, a Jobber dashboard, Keith at the desk with the route map up, and shop / yard footage from the cohort grad's actual operations once a cycle has run.

Cold Open (00:00 - 00:30)

On camera, Keith, work shirt, at the desk:

"If you have watched my videos, you have already seen me talk about the trap - bidding too low, hiring the wrong guy, working 60 windshield hours a week and wondering why the revenue is stuck. This is what the cohort is. 8 weeks. 20 to 25 operators. Four installs. The cohort is the install version of the podcast - not another module library."

Why The Cohort Now (00:30 - 01:30)

The Alliance does the community job at $97 a month. The courses cover the content. The 1:1 coaching call is the top of the stack. What is missing is the structured group product for the operator who has already watched the videos, bought the bundle, listened to the podcast in the truck, and is now stuck at the same revenue number two years running.

The cohort sits between the Alliance and the 1:1. 8 weeks. Four modules:

  • The bid - week 1-2
  • The first crew lead - week 3-4
  • Route economics - week 5-6
  • Owner-out-of-the-truck plan - week 7-8

Weekly group call. Office hours. Real implementation on the operator's real business. Not theory.

What Gets Installed By Week 4 (01:30 - 03:00)

By the end of week 2, the bid script is fixed. We walk the operator's last 3 estimates on the live call. The per-property pricing matrix gets locked. The upsell ladder is built. The next 3 estimates the operator runs after week 2 use the new script. The deals land or the deals walk - they do not get bid at the old rate.

By the end of week 4, the first crew lead is hired. The job ad is written together on the call. The interview script is run live. The pay structure is decided. The 30-60-90 onboarding plan is documented. The operator who entered the cohort solo or with one body in the truck leaves week 4 with a crew lead onboarding plan in hand.

These are not lessons. These are decisions made and paperwork signed during the 8 weeks.

What Gets Installed By Week 8 (03:00 - 04:30)

By the end of week 6, route economics are locked. The route map is drawn. The windshield-time-per-dollar number gets calculated for the operator's actual route. The de-route plan - which stops drop, which clients get fired, which sub-routes get consolidated - is documented.

By the end of week 8, the owner-out-of-the-truck plan is written. 12-month sequence with a revenue checkpoint at each stage. The cohort grad leaves with a plan to remove himself from production in a known timeframe - not "someday."

That is the install. Four operator decisions made during the cohort window, each one walked through on a group call with office-hours support.

Who It's For - And Who It's Not (04:30 - 05:30)

This is for: landscaping, lawn care, window cleaning, pressure washing operators with at least $80K annual revenue and at least 12 months in business. Operators who have watched the videos, listened to the podcast, bought the bundle, and want the install version. Operators ready to do real implementation work in 8 weeks.

This is not for: pre-revenue startup operators (start with the free YouTube series and the $97 marketing course - they are free and they work). Operators looking for done-for-you services (the cohort is mentorship, you do the work). Multi-truck operators past $1M revenue who already have systems documented (a 1:1 coaching call is the right fit for narrow questions).

Investment + What's Included (05:30 - 06:30)

Standard tier covers the 8-week cohort, weekly group calls, scheduled office hours, the private community channel, and all cohort templates - the bid script, the interview script, the route map worksheet, the owner-out-of-truck planner. Premium tier adds a 1:1 business audit call and 3 follow-up 30-minute check-ins in the 90 days post-cohort. No upsells past that. Cohort cap of 20 to 25 is hard.

Close (06:30 - 07:00)

Application is below the video. 4 minutes to fill in. Cohort cycles run quarterly. Alliance members get priority application.

"If the videos are the marketing, the cohort is the install. The application is below. - Keith."

Production Notes

  • Filming location: Keith's office (talking head) + truck loading B-roll at sunrise + yard/shop footage + Jobber dashboard screen-record
  • Camera: Whatever Keith already uses for YouTube long-form - matches the existing channel feel, no production-value uncanny-valley
  • Wardrobe: work shirt or Keith's existing branded shirt - same look as the YouTube channel
  • Lighting: key + fill for office talking head, natural light at sunrise for truck B-roll
  • Pace: target 1.0x watchable at all times. No long pauses. B-roll cuts every 12-18 seconds during talking-head sections.
  • On-screen graphics: bid sheet, route map, Jobber screenshot, interview script - lower-third overlays during the relevant sections
  • Music: none under talking head. Light bed under B-roll only. Royalty-free.
  • Captions: burnt-in for IG / TikTok / YouTube shorts; SRT for long-form Wistia
  • Thumbnail (Wistia poster): Keith at desk, route map on screen, looking at camera. Title overlay: "The 8-week cohort for the operator past free content."

Short Cuts (for paid traffic + IG / TikTok / podcast clips)

From the long-form, edit: - 1 x 3-min cohort overview cut (cold ad asset) - 3 x 60-90 sec hook-led trailers (the bid trap / the hiring trap / the route trap) - 8 x 30-45 sec module-specific cuts (one per cohort module x 2) - 6 x 15-20 sec IG reel hooks pulled from cold open + week-4 install moment - 4 x 20-30 sec podcast intermission cuts ("if this episode landed, the cohort is the install version - link in show notes")

Distribution

  • Long-form Wistia · landing-page hero
  • 3-min cut · YouTube + cold paid traffic
  • 60-90 sec trailers · YouTube shorts + IG reels + TikTok (top of funnel)
  • 30-45 sec module cuts · IG / TikTok content calendar (Phase 3)
  • 15-20 sec hooks · TikTok + IG reels
  • 20-30 sec podcast intermissions · spliced into next 10 episodes of The Untrapped Podcast as mid-roll
