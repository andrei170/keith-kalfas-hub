Phase 3

Content Strategy - Keith Kalfas

Phase 3 deliverable · Hook bank, podcast plan, photoshoot brief, IG / YouTube / TikTok short-form roadmap.

Premise

Keith's audience across IG, YouTube, TikTok, X, FB, and The Untrapped Podcast catalogue is engaged but undirected past the $97-$347 product band. The content delivers operator depth on landscaping pricing, hiring, route economics, and getting off the mower - but no piece of content currently routes a viewer to a specific lead-capture event that segments by operator stage. Phase 3 builds a content layer with one job: get the right viewer onto the Landscaping Business Trap Score and onto the Cohort Waitlist.

Podcast Plan

Current state: The Untrapped Podcast With Keith Kalfas is on every major platform with hundreds of episodes. The content is excellent. The end-card is currently not productised - either no CTA, a generic "go to keithkalfas.com," or an affiliate plug.

Phase 3 move: turn the existing podcast into the warmest top-of-funnel asset in the funnel.

  1. End-card refresh - rotate two end-cards across upcoming episodes: - "If this episode landed, the cohort is the install version - the Cohort Waitlist link is in the show notes." - "Score your operator trap in the next 90 seconds - link in the show notes."
  2. Mid-roll intermissions - 20-30 second spliced reads of Keith pointing to the scorecard or the cohort waitlist, baked into the next 10 episodes
  3. Show-notes template - every episode notes page links to the Cohort Waitlist + the Trap Score + the affiliate stack (in that order)
  4. 6-episode "Cohort Module Walkthrough" mini-series - one episode per module of the cohort (pricing, hiring, route, owner-out-of-truck) plus a "who the cohort is for" episode and a "what I would do at year 1 vs year 5" episode. Each episode 30-45 minutes. Pulls scorecard sign-ups + cohort waitlist applications from listeners who already trust Keith's voice.

Why: zero new top-of-funnel filming pressure on Keith because the podcast is already the top of funnel. The work is one filming day for the mid-roll reads plus the 6-episode mini-series (which doubles as YouTube long-form for the channel).

Photoshoot Brief

Two-day shoot, mid-cycle in Phase 3 (Week 7-8 ideal).

Day 1 - Yard / Shop · The Operator Setup: Keith loading the truck at sunrise, walking a route stop, talking to a crew lead, on the phone running an estimate, at the desk with the route map up, Jobber dashboard on screen. Real work-environment, work-shirt, work-truck. Natural light, no commercial-photo gloss. 200+ stills, 3-5 hours of B-roll.

Day 2 - Cohort-Specific · The Cohort Setup: Keith at the desk on a Zoom call (cohort group call B-roll), with whiteboards, with cohort templates printed out, on a phone office-hours call. Studio-style office, single key light + fill, slight grade up for the cohort-specific shots so they read as the "implementation" layer of the funnel. 150+ stills, 2-3 hours of B-roll.

Outputs feed: cohort landing page, scorecard result page, 40-hook IG / TikTok content calendar, ad creative library, YouTube end-card cohort CTA, podcast cover art refresh, hub page.

Short-Form Roadmap (90 days)

  • Weeks 4-6: post 5 hooks/week from Categories 1-3 (pricing, hiring, route) - the highest-intent operator-pain content
  • Weeks 7-9: post 5/week from Categories 4-6 (owner-out-of-truck, trap stories, scorecard-ready hooks) - bridges from operator-pain to the scorecard
  • Weeks 10-12: post 5/week from Categories 7-8 (cohort, operator story) + retargeting ads layered on top of organic

Cadence: 5 IG reels + 5 TikTok mirrors + 5 YouTube shorts + 1 long-form / week. Repurpose ratio 1:5 - one filmed asset = one long-form episode + 5 short cuts across IG / TikTok / YouTube shorts.

Conversion Goal

Every piece of content has the same final CTA: "Landscaping Business Trap Score - score your operator bottleneck in under 2 minutes, get the trap that is costing you the most right now in your inbox" - with the link in bio routing through Beacons / Linktree to the scorecard quiz. The cohort waitlist sits one click in from the scorecard result page.
